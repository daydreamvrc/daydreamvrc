About us

We are looking for dedicated social VR players to join us in
our mission to change the game. We believe that the current
state of the market is unacceptable, with overpriced models, subpar
creators, and rampant greed. We support creators who put in the effort
to create exceptional avatars. If we love their work, we show our appreciation
by making a purchase.


We are not evil. We do not “leak” assets—we simply share them with each other.
“Leaking” entails malicious intent, of which we do not have.
We are not a “freeavatar” group, we are not a “steal from the creators” group,
we are a group that wants to see change.
